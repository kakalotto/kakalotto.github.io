drwuvideos email reminder app

Usage

The purpose of this app is to email students when their assignments are late.

Even though Google classroom allows one to see what is missing or late, an email from the teacher will sometimes engender action that would not otherwise happen.


This functionality of this app is that the app scans a Google classroom using Google classroom API, and emails students a status report about their missing asignments using Gmail API.
